Do You Know??

Agriculture contributes around 17% to India's GDP. It was ahead of industrial contribution to the country's economy in 2019-20.

Farmers are the pillars of our nation. They work hard to supply food items for everyone living in the nation throughout their lives.In India,nearly two-thirds of the population rely on agriculture for their livelihood. However,agriculture is a challenging domain that faces various challenges such as weather conditions,transport facilities,storing harvested crops, and price fluctuations to sell the harvested crop.It mainly focuses on helping farmers by accessing the basic information which is needed by them in advance that can help them save their crop,provide access to various facilities, and ease their tasks by bringing facilities to their fingertips. The platform provides farmers with real-time weather updates, crop health monitoring, pest and disease management, and soil analysis. It also provides access to various agricultural schemes, loans, subsidies, and government policies.It is an easy-to-use platform that is accessible to farmers from remote areas with limited internet connectivity. The platform is available in multiple languages and works on low-end devices. It aims to empower farmers with the necessary information and tools to make informed decisions and improve their agricultural practices. It aims to make farming a profitable and sustainable profession and improve the overall livelihood of farmers.
